<pre>
I see you across the table—
sprigs of rosemary
decorating everyone's drinks,
adorning some dishes.
Ever festive,
your unique herbal scent.
Evergreen branches
from some shrub
of unknown origin,
perfuming this night's gathering.
</pre>
