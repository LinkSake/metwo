<pre>
At the center of the park
like many in the city
there is a kiosk:
sheet metal roof
failed Mediterranean architecture
colorful paint
political images on the metal

Castle for children
shelter for adolescents
shade for adults
refuge for poets

So universal
I forgot to stop
to remember how important they are
</pre>
