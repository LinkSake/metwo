6:45

December 8, 2025 · 39 words · 1 minutes · by Luis Angel Ortega 

A yellow and orange crown
behind the hills
a layer of mist
halfway up them —
birdsong
cold smells of grass
gray and blue colors
filling my lungs —
children cross the park
on their way to school —
